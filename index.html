Frequently Asked Questions

What is student counselling?

Student Counseling Is A Professional Service That Focuses On Providing Support and Guidance to Students in Various Aspects of Their Academic and Personal Lives.

What Does Student Counseling Entail?

Student Counseling Encompasses a Wide Range of Services Aimed at Addressing Students' Academic, Emotional, and Social Needs. These Services Include Providing Guidance on Academic Planning, Offering Support for Mental Health Concerns, and Assisting with Personal Development and Goal Setting.

How Can Student Counseling Help?

Student Counseling Aims to Support Students in Overcoming Challenges and Achieving Their Academic and Personal Goals. Through Individual or Group Sessions, Counselors Provide a Safe and Confidential Space for Students to Explore Their Concerns, Develop Coping Strategies, and Enhance Their Overall Well-being.
